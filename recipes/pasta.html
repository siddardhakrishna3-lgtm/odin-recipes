Pasta

DESCRIPTION

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine, available in hundreds of shapes and prepared with various sauces ranging from creamy carbonara to spicy arrabbiata.

INGREDIENTS

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • salt
  • butter
  • onion
  • tomatoes
  • parmesan cheese
  • herbs

STEPS

  1. Boil Water: Fill a large pot with water and add a big spoonful of salt. Wait for it to bubble rapidly.
  2. Add Pasta: Put the pasta in. Give it a quick stir so it doesn't stick together
  3. Test It: Cook for about 8 to 10 minutes (check the box for the exact time). Taste a pieceâ€”it should be soft but still a little firm (this is called al dente).
  4. Drain & Mix: Pour the pasta into a colander to get rid of the water. Toss it immediately with your sauce so it doesn't get dry.
