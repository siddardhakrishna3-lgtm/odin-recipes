LASAGNA

DESCRIPTION

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of stacked layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, alternating with rich sauces, cheeses, and other fillings, which is then baked until golden and bubbly.

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta
  • Sause
  • Chesse
  • Herbs & Seasonings

STEPS

  1. Prep the Sauce: Brown 1 lb ground beef with some onion and garlic, then stir in a 24 oz jar of marinara sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Mix the Cheese: In a bowl, stir together 15 oz ricotta, 1 egg, and a handful of Parmesan.
  3. Layer the Dish: In a 9x13 pan, layer in this order: Thin layer of sauce. Noodles Ricotta mix Sauce Shredded Mozzarella. Repeat until the pan is full, ending with plenty of cheese on top
  4. Bake and Rest: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes until bubbly. Wait 15 minutes before cutting so it doesn't fall apart.
